The Senate of College Councils is proud to offer the Non-Traditional Student Scholarship to eligible UT Austin students. For this scholarship, a non-traditional student is considered any undergraduate student who is one or more of the following:

- External transfer student
- A veteran
- A student over the age of 25 pursuing their first bachelor's degree
- An international student
- A first-generation student
- A student who is a parent

Students must meet the following criteria to be eligible to apply:

Must have attended UT Austin for at least two long semesters
Have maintained or currently have a cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
Be considered a 'non-traditional' student as outlined above
Students who are currently serving as a member of the Senate of the College Councils are not eligible to apply.
